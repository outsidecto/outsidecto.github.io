About/Contact

Outside CTO, LLC, is dedicated to helping small companies get their early stage technology story straight. We're based in Minneapolis and Boston, but can help you pretty much anywhere.

Our speciality is in providing guidance to early stage businesses. We can help you with hiring, with establishing a strong engineering culture and process, as well in as specific domains such as stack selection, security, and compliance. Our security and compliance work focuses on HIPAA, and especially early stage "HIPAA from Scratch."

Outside CTO's proprietor is John G. Norman, who serves as the Vice President of Technology and Information Security at Iora Health. Iora's tech team has done some crazy things: It built its own electronic medical records (EHR) system, and over some nine years only saw a couple of engineers leave. It was built for the long term, and that's the kind of intervention John can bring to your early stage effort.

The creation of Outside CTO was inspired by the example of the company Outside GC (general counsel), which was founded by John's friend Jon Levitt. An "outside" CTO provides a different service than that of an "outside" GC, but early stage technical guidance has always seemed to be a gap in the consulting marketplace.

John keeps a personal blog at 7fff.com.
